# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want MakerCommons to be a place where anyone — regardless of experience,
background, or identity — can bring a problem or help solve one without fear of
being belittled or harassed. We pledge to make participation a welcoming,
respectful experience for everyone.

## Our standards

**Examples of behavior that builds the commons:**

- Being kind, patient, and welcoming — especially to newcomers.
- Assuming good faith and giving constructive feedback.
- Respecting that people volunteer their time here.
- Gracefully accepting that "no" or "not this way" is sometimes the answer.
- Focusing on what's best for the people we're building for.

**Examples of unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments, public or private.
- Discrimination or demeaning language of any kind.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Sustained disruption, trolling, or bad-faith argument.
- Gatekeeping — making people feel unwelcome for not knowing enough.

## Scope

This applies in all MakerCommons spaces — repos, issues, pull requests,
discussions, chat — and when representing the collective in public.

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to the stewards by
opening a confidential report (email address to be added once available) or by
contacting a steward directly. Reports will be handled discreetly and in good
faith.

Stewards may warn, temporarily suspend, or permanently remove anyone whose
behavior they judge harmful to the community. We favor de-escalation and
education first — but the wellbeing of the community comes before any one
person's participation.
